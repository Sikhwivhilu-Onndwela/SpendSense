Our Story

About SpendSense

A platform born from a real problem that every South African student knows.

Why this exists

Every month, millions of South African students receive an allowance — from parents, from NSFAS, from part-time jobs. And every month, that money runs out before it should. Not because they do not work hard. Not because they do not care. But because nobody ever taught them how to manage it.

SpendSense was built to change that. It is a simple, free platform that gives students the tools to see, understand and improve their spending — starting with the very first rand.

Who it is for

  • University and college students on a tight budget
  • High school learners learning to manage pocket money
  • Young adults starting their first part-time job
  • Anyone in South Africa wanting to stretch their rands further

Always Free

No subscriptions, no hidden costs. Financial literacy should be accessible to everyone.

Made for SA

Every tip and every rand amount is tailored to the South African student reality.

No Data Collected

We do not store your numbers. Everything stays in your session. Your finances, your business.

Built to Grow

Future versions will include savings goals, NSFAS budgeting tools and more.

"Financial freedom begins with financial awareness. SpendSense gives every South African student that first step."
